LeafSkim

LeafSkim An IoT-Based Smart Leaf Skimmer System LeafSkim is an IoT project designed to control and monitor an automated leaf skimming machine. The system allows users to remotely turn the machine on and off, receive alerts, and view operational data in real time. The machine collects floating leaves from the water surface into a basket. When the basket becomes full and heavy enough to trigger a limit switch, the water pump automatically stops. The system then sends a notification to the user, indicating that the basket is full. In case of system malfunctions or abnormal behavior, users are immediately alerted. The system also logs data every time the basket is full, including the date, time, and duration of each cleaning session. I was responsible for hardware design and engineering in this project.
